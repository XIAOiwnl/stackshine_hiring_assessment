For the homepage of the application, the application must display the following:

  • Name of the “Workspace”
  • Domain of the “Workspace”
  • A Link to the “Members” of the “Workspace”
  • A Link to the “Workspace Vendors” of the “Workspace”

Upon clicking on the “Workspace Vendors” link, the application will redirect into a separate page and see a list of “Workspace Vendors’’ for that specific “Workspace”. The page must show a list of the names and descriptions of all the “Workspace Vendors”. Each of “Workspace Vendor’s” names will be a link that will redirect to a page that displays the “Workspace Vendor’s” “name”, “description”, and its “vendor transactions”.

Upon clicking on the “Members” link, the application will redirect into a separate page and see a list of “Members” for that specific “Workspace”. The page must show a list of the emails of all the “Members”. Each of “Member’s” names will be a link that will redirect to a page that displays the “Member’s” “first” and “last name”, “email”, and “created_at” date.

Note: We prioritize correctness of the data shown rather than the “prettiness” of the application. We strongly encourage you to not focus on the visuals of the application (other than the bare minimum for legibility) but rather the correctness of the user flow and data shown.
